About(game)

Roselia is an all-girls band from the Bang Dream! franchise.
They are a gothic J-rock band and are acknowledged as professionals in the music industry,
known for their alternative/gothic rock music and stunning gothic visuals
It consists of Minato Yukina(Vo.), Hikawa Sayo(Gtr), Imai Lisa(Bas), Udagawa Ako(Drm) and Shirokane Rinko(Key).

About(real life)

The real-life band consists of Aina Aiba (Vo.), Haruka Kudo (Gtr), Yuki Nakashima (Bas), Megu Sakuragawa (Drm), and Kanon Shizaki (Key).
They are the voice actresses of the girls, and perform using the same instrument as their voiced character
Roselia is one of the six bands that perform live, out of the eleven bands in the game
